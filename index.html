About Greenfield

Greenfield School is a community-centered independent school focused on inquiry-based learning. Our faculty are dedicated educators who personalize instruction and encourage students to lead, explore, and collaborate.

Programs

Early Childhood

Play-based curriculum for ages 3–5 that builds language, social skills, and a love for discovery.

Lower School

Strong foundations in literacy and numeracy with integrated arts and physical education.

Middle School

Project-driven learning, leadership opportunities, and preparation for high school success.
